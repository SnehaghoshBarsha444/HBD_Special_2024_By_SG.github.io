HAPPY BIRTHDAY

Respected Sir,

To Our Amit Sir


Happy Birthday, Sir, it’s your day to shine,
We’re all here to say, hope it’s truly divine!
From lectures and lessons to jokes on the side,
You’ve taught us with passion, with wisdom as your guide.

You make learning fun, in your own clever style,
Keeping us engaged, with laughter and a smile.
Today’s for you, with cheer and applause,
For the work you do without a pause.

So here’s to you, with all our best,
A well-earned break, some joy, and rest.
Happy Birthday, Sir, may your year be bright,
With success, laughter, and pure delight!

- By A Grateful Student
